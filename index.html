Key Features

Player vs Player Mode

Challenge your friends in a classic game of Tic-Tac-Toe. Compete against each other to see who can get three in a row first.

Player vs AI Mode

Play against a computer AI with varying difficulty levels. Choose from easy, medium, or hard mode for a challenging solo gaming experience.

About

This project was inspired by my childhood memories of playing Tic-Tac-Toe with friends and family. I have always been fascinated by the simplicity and yet the depth of strategy involved in this classic game. As I embarked on my journey in software development, I wanted to recreate this timeless game and challenge myself to implement an AI opponent that could provide a competitive gaming experience.

The development timeline for this project spanned several weeks, from initial concept and planning to implementation and testing. This project is part of my portfolio for Holberton School, where I am honing my skills as a software engineer.
